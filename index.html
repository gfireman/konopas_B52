    Welcome to Balticon 56!

    The Baltimore Science Fiction Society is holding Balticon 56 as a hybrid in-person/virtual convention Memorial Day weekend, May 27 — 30, 2022.

    Program and participant data are subject to change. All times are listed in EDT.

    This guide will work in your browser even when you don't have an internet connection.

    Home screen app install instructions

    Safari on iPhone/iPad
    Tap on (share), then tap Add to Home Screen.
    Chrome on Android
    Tap on (menu), then tap Add to Home Screen.
    Firefox on Android
    Tap on (menu), then tap Bookmark and select Options, and Add to Home Screen.
    IE on Windows Phone
    Tap on (menu), then tap Pin to Start
