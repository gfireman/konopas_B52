    Welcome to Balticon 55!

    The Baltimore Science Fiction Society is holding Balticon 55 as a free virtual convention Memorial Day weekend, May 28-31, 2021. Please consider helping with expenses by donating here.

    Program and participant data are subject to change. All times are listed in Eastern Daylight Time. Last update: .

    This guide is an instance of KonOpas, a mobile-friendly guide for conventions, with all sorts of spiffy features. It can be used on most browsers and devices, and will work even when you don’t have an internet connection.
